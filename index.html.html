Inspiring Journeys

Arjun came from a small village with big dreams but little guidance. After his 10th grade, he attended a counseling session by Shree Ganesha Mithra Mandal, which ignited his interest in technology. With the Mandal's support, he chose the MPC stream, studied hard for his entrance exams, and secured a spot in a reputable engineering college. Today, Arjun is a successful Software Engineer at a leading multinational tech company, proving that with the right guidance, any goal is achievable.

Priya always wanted to help people but was intimidated by the competitive medical field. Our counselors worked with her, helping her understand the path to becoming a doctor and building her confidence. She took the BiPC stream and, with consistent effort and the study resources provided by us, she cleared the NEET exam. Dr. Priya now serves in a government hospital in her hometown, fulfilling her dream of making a difference in her community.

Rohan was a creative student who didn't fit into the traditional Science or Commerce molds. He was passionate about filmmaking and storytelling. The Mandal's aptitude tests highlighted his creative strengths and suggested a career in media. Instead of a conventional degree, he enrolled in a professional course for Video Editing and Animation. Now, Rohan is a sought-after freelance video editor, working with popular content creators and production houses, turning his passion into a thriving career.
